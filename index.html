Software Development portfolio

Programming quickly became a part of who I am once I started in February 2023. I strongly believe the future of our economy is in tech and have shifted my focus accordingly. I started out coding in Python, building apps and games, then switched to web development with Django, JavaScript, HTML, CSS and incorporating some SQL databases. Since then I've spent most of my time in Java, but have also familiarised myself with Kotlin, AWS, React and basic data science. Taking this multi-faceted approach has given me a broader understanding of development concepts than if I had just stuck with one language.

My previous work in the music industry taught me a lot about being in a team. It also provided plenty of opportunity to study tech from an audio perspective. Getting things to work has always been an obsession for me, from recording cassette tapes to modular synths to mixing in digital audio workstations like Ableton Live. I pride myself on attention to detail and am not someone who cuts corners. I'm hard-working, committed and want those around me to thrive.
