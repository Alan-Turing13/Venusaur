Dom Marshall

Programming is a passion for me and has been a key part of who I am since I started in February 2023. I pride myself on attention to detail and am not someone who cuts corners. I am hard-working, focused and want those around me to thrive. Working in the music industry has taught me a lot about being in a team. It has also provided a lot of opportunities to study tech from an audio perspective. Getting things to work has always been an obsession for me, from recording cassette tapes to modular synths to mixing with digital audio workstations.

I now believe the future of our economy is in tech and have shifted my focus over to software development. For the first few months of my study I focused on Python, building numerous apps and games, then shifted my attention towards web development using JavaScript, HTML, CSS and Django. Since then I have branched out to learning Java, Kotlin, AWS, React and basic data science. I also built a website for my music portfolio using some Google Cloud and PHP.
