    Although I didn't dedicate myself to it until later in life, programming quickly became part of who I am once I started. Having pursued my dream of being a jazz musician, I decided to shift my focus according to the future of our economy, which is in tech.

    Coming from the creative world, I was pleasantly surprised how satisfying it is getting code to work. Beginning with Python, building apps and games, each problem solved felt like I had solved a mystery. Before long I expanded to web development with Django, JS, HTML, CSS and database logic. I experimented with React, AWS and Kotlin, and then found my way to Java, which is where I spend most of my time now. (Thanks to the compiler, my tendency to want to throw my computer at the wall has significantly decreased.) I'm thankful for some great teachers online who made this knowledge available to me.
