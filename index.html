Dom Marshall

Programming quickly became a part of who I am once I started in February 2023. I strongly believe the future of our economy is in tech and have shifted my focus accordingly. For the first few months of study I focused on Python, building numerous apps and games, then switched to web development using JavaScript, HTML, CSS and (incorporating SQL databases) Django. Since then I branched out to learning Java fundamentals, Kotlin, AWS, React and basic data science, which have given me a broader understanding of development concepts.

My previous work in the music industry taught me a lot about being in a team. It also provided plenty of opportunity to study tech from an audio perspective. Getting machines to work has always been an obsession, from recording cassette tapes to modular synths to mixing in digital audio workstations like Ableton Live. I pride myself on attention to detail and am not someone who cuts corners. I am hard-working, focused and want those around me to thrive.
